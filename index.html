How to play:
  1. Create items in your assigned region.
  2. The top item crafts for free, everything else is crafted from other items.
  3. Select the sell checkbox to sell items for cash.
  4. Buy upgrades with cash to improve creation speed.
  5. Crafting higher level items requires items from other regions.
  6. Export your items and send the export code to others (via external social media).
  7. Use the pause crafting button to stock up on items below prior to export.
  8. Import codes from others to increase production rates.
  9. Try to create the final item in your region!
